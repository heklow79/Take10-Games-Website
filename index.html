Take10 Games is a production studio specializing in the art of character animation.

Our focus is on 3D and traditional animation for tv, film, games, and other related products.

Take10 Games is dedicated to producing high-quality character animation on time and on budget. Although we think motion capture is great, we are a better choice for key-frame animation. Let Take10 Games guide you through an enjoyable experience in bringing your characters to life.

What we do:

Spine is an animation tool that focuses specifically on 2D animation for games. Spine aims to have an efficient, streamlined workflow, both for creating animations using the editor and for making use of those animations in games using the Spine Runtimes.

Maya is the industry standard software for 3D visual effects, character creation, rigging, and animation.

The industry standard for digital sculpting and painting.

Substance 3D Painter is widely used in game and movie production. It's a go-to app for texturing your 3D assets.

Unreal Engine 5 is the highly popular game engine capable of creating "next-gen" games.
